Parmesan potatoes
Cut the potatoes into small cubes.
Add some butter to a frying pan and heat up.
Add potatoes to frying pan, salt them and fry over medium heat for about
20 minutes moving the potatoes from time to time.
About 3-5 minutes before removing the potatoes from the heat, add some
rosemary leaves a stir the potatoes.
Remove potatoes from pan, serve and add shredded Parmesan and some freshly
ground pepper on top.
Enjoy!
